## SECTION E: KEY MARKET INSIGHTS

### India Orthopaedic Market Facts
- **Meril Life Sciences** holds ~45% of the Indian orthopaedic implant market (domestic leader)
- **Alkem Laboratories** acquired Bombay Ortho Industries (Feb 2025, Rs 147 Cr) and partnered with Exactech (Jan 2025) - signaling pharma entry into ortho devices
- **Gujarat (Ahmedabad/Rajkot/Vadodara)** is the manufacturing hub for most Indian ortho implant companies
- **New Delhi** is the base for several large manufacturers (GPC, Narang, Siora)
- Most Indian manufacturers hold CE + ISO 13485 certifications; several have US FDA 510(k)

### Global Market Share (Joints)
- **Knee:** Zimmer Biomet (33%), Stryker (29%), DePuy/J&J (16%), Smith & Nephew (11%)
- **Hip:** Zimmer Biomet (25%), Stryker (24%), DePuy/J&J (21%), Smith & Nephew (9%)
- **Spine:** Medtronic (32%), Globus Medical/NuVasive (23%)

### Product Categories Available
1. **Trauma** - Bone plates (locking, DCP, reconstruction), screws, interlocking nails, K-wires, external fixators, DHS/DCS
2. **Spine** - Pedicle screws, rods, cages (TLIF/PLIF/ALIF), cervical plates, PEEK implants
3. **Joints/Arthroplasty** - Total knee replacement, total hip replacement, shoulder, elbow, revision systems
4. **Sports Medicine** - Arthroscopy implants, ACL reconstruction, rotator cuff anchors
5. **Maxillofacial/CMF** - Plates, screws, mesh for facial fractures
6. **External Fixation** - Ilizarov, hybrid, AO-type fixators

## SECTION F: HOW TO FIND DISTRIBUTORS FOR PONDICHERRY / TAMIL NADU

1. **IndiaMART** (dir.indiamart.com) - Search "orthopaedic implants Tamil Nadu" for local dealers
2. **ExportersIndia** (exportersindia.com/tamil-nadu/orthopaedic-implant.htm) - 73+ products listed
3. **Direct contact** - Most manufacturers have "Become a Dealer" or distribution inquiry forms on their websites
4. **Hospital procurement departments** - Major hospitals in Chennai/Pondicherry (JIPMER, AIIMS, Apollo, etc.) can share their approved vendor lists
5. **CDSCO database** - Check registered medical device importers/manufacturers at cdsco.gov.in
